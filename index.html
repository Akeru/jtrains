JTrains

Free, offline japanese railways route finder for Android.

Features

Free

No ads, no crippled/lite version.

Offline

No network required.

Comprehensive

Covers 630+ lines and 9000+ stations.

Localized

Full stations and lines readings provided.

Compatible

Runs even on Android 2.1.

Japan Railways Mode

Use with the Japan Railpass.
